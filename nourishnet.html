▸ Process

Simple steps, real impact

01

List or Discover

Providers upload surplus food with photos, quantity, and expiry. Consumers browse by category, distance, or availability type.

02

Choose Your Mode

Donate freely, sell at a discount, or contribute via reward points. Consumers access based on eligibility.

03

Coordinate Pickup

Self-pickup, self-delivery, or assign a volunteer. Real-time tracking keeps everyone informed.

04

Earn & Redeem

Providers earn reward points for donations. Redeem for food items, essentials, or platform credits.
