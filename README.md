# 🍽️ Food Redistribution Platform {RE-SERVE} – README

## 📌 Overview

This platform is designed to eliminate food waste by creating a smart bridge between food providers and people in need. It combines donation, affordability, and incentive systems into one unified ecosystem.

---

## 🎯 Problem Statement

A large amount of food is wasted daily from restaurants, events, households, and supermarkets, while many individuals and communities struggle to access basic meals.

---

## 💡 Solution

We provide a digital platform where:

* Excess food is redistributed efficiently
* People can access food for free or at minimal cost
* Contributors are rewarded for reducing food waste

---

## 🔁 How It Works

### 🧭 Step 1: Role-Based Entry (NEW FLOW)

When users enter the platform, they first choose how they want to participate:

* **Provider** – Share excess food
* **Consumer** – Receive food
* **Volunteer** – Help deliver food
* **Sponsor** – Donate money

This ensures a clear, intent-driven user experience.

---

### 📂 Step 2: Category Selection

After selecting a role, users choose a category:

* Food

  * Meals
  * Bakery
  * Chats
  * Beverages
* Nearly Expiring Grocery
* Fruits & Vegetables

---

### ⚙️ Step 3: Action Based on Role

#### 🧑‍🍳 Providers

Upload surplus food and choose:

* Donate for free (earn reward points)
* Sell at a minimal price
* Offer reward-based listings

---

#### 🍴 Consumers

Access food by:

* Receiving it for free
* Paying a small amount
* Redeeming reward points

---

#### 🚚 Volunteers

* Accept delivery tasks
* Deliver food to receivers
* Earn reward points

---

#### 💰 Sponsors

* Donate money to support food distribution
* Sponsor meals for people in need
* Track their social impact

---

## ⭐ Reward System

* Providers earn points for donating food for free
* Volunteers earn points for completing deliveries
* Users who contributed earlier can redeem points later

### 🎁 Redemption Options:

* Food items
* Essential products available on the platform

---

## 💰 Donation Model

* Users can donate money directly
* Funds are used to:

  * Purchase discounted food
  * Provide it free to those in need
* Volunteers are assigned to handle delivery

---

## 🚚 Delivery System

Food distribution can happen through:

* Self pickup
* Self delivery
* Volunteers (reward-based)
* Student workers (points or money)
* Paid delivery partners

---
